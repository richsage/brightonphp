On 18th February, BrightonPHP and Drupal Brighton</a> joined forces and met at The Skiff</a> for an evening of Drupal-based presentations. Since we both meet on the same night around 200 yards away from each other, it seemed a natural thing to combine forces for one night!</p>

Steve Purkiss</a> got things off to a great start with a brief history of the Drupal project, some history about Drupal Brighton and a good overview of Drupal for those perhaps not so familiar with it. Jeni Tehan</a> then took over the baton and demonstrated the power and flexibility of Drupal with a few clicks of the mouse, creating a backend for an example music review site. Jeni presents the "Tasty Backend" series of talks at various Drupal conferences, and it was great to see some real-world examples of Drupal in action and how easy it can be to get a basic management area up and running quickly.</p>

Following the presentations, much discussion ensued, with the evening ending at the North Laine</a> for drinks and further discussion.</p>

Hopefully this can be the first of many collaborations with Drupal Brighton - thanks for coming along!</p>

Our next meeting is on Monday 18th March 2013</strong>, 7pm for a 7.15pm start</p>
