Also thanks to those of you that came to Stu Herbert's talk at the last meeting - it provoked some interesting discussion and Q&A afterwards! Stu spoke about how DataSift use PHP to handle (in various methods) every piece of data that comes into the DataSift application. DataSift are one of only three companies worldwide to have access to the full Twitter firehose, so this is large large amounts of data. It was interesting to see how PHP managed this, and the way in which DataSift employ PHP alongside other technologies to deliver real-time information and statistics.</p>
