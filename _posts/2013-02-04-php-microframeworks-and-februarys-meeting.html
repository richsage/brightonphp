A belated happy new year! January's meeting took place on the 21st Jan at The Skiff</a>, where we had a presentation by Emmanuel Ide</a> on the state of PHP microframeworks. Emmanuel started off by giving us some background on frameworks within PHP and then moved onto the requirement for a micro framework. He covered some code examples including some from a real-world application, and then rounded off with some pointers to further information, and a good Q&A at the end.

If you're interested, Emmanuel's slides are available over at Slideshare</a>.

Following the meeting we headed to The North Laine</a> pub/microbrewery a few doors down from the venue. It was good to see a few new faces this month also!

February's presentation will take place on Monday 18th February 2013, again at The Skiff. This month we're joined by the Drupal Brighton</a> members, who'll present a few talks on Drupal and the project's current status and direction, why people have moved to Drupal from vanilla PHP/MySQL and similar talks. This should be a great set of presentations! Following the presentation and discussions, we'll head to the pub.
